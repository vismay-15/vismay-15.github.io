Background

Engineer. Educator.
Mentor.

I am a Civil Engineer, Construction Manager, and Academic with 10 years of experience across infrastructure delivery, higher education teaching, and digital learning. I hold an MSc in Civil Engineering with Distinction from the University of West London and a BE Civil Engineering with Merit from Gujarat Technological University.

Currently serving as Lecturer in Engineering at the University of East London through Malvern International PLC, I deliver modules in Construction Technology, Project Management, Sustainability, and Applied Engineering Maths. I also teach NCUK Foundation Year programmes, supporting students' preparation for university progression.

As Research Lead at the University of West London, I focus on the Water-Energy-Food (WEF) Nexus and Digital Twin development for sustainable infrastructure — integrating IoT and AI for real-time monitoring of constructed wetland systems. My industry experience includes managing £15M+ construction projects and leading site operations with full HSE compliance.
