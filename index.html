Who I Am

Engineer. Educator.
Mentor.

I am a Geotechnical Engineer, Lecturer, and Research Lead with over 10 years of professional experience spanning civil engineering consultancy, academic teaching, and sustainability-focused research. I hold an MSc in Civil Engineering with Distinction from the University of West London.

Currently serving as Lecturer in Construction and Programme Manager at Caspian School of Academics (London), I lead delivery of undergraduate construction modules and mentor students on academic progression and career planning in the built environment sector.

As Research Lead on the Water-Energy-Food (WEF) Nexus in Europe, I focus on governance models, stakeholder collaboration, and digital twin solutions for sustainable infrastructure — bridging research and real-world impact.
